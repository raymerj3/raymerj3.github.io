All about Flight Sims!



What is a Flight Simulator?

Logically, a flight simulator is a system that is used to replicate the experience of being an airplane pilot. There are two main types of flight simulators: professional pilot training simulators and the personal flight sim, that comes as a computer program to be used on a personal computer. Professional pilot training simulators are used to train pilots on a specific aircraft type. Along with being a flight simulator program, they feature a hyper-relistic cockpit with controls identical to the aircraft being trained on and often sit on hydraulic jacks which can provide g-force and simulate turbulence similar to realistic flight.


List of Some of the Most Popular Flight Sims:

Microsoft Flight Simulator

Released: 2020. A very well rounded simulator with hyper-realistic graphics. Great for beginners and pros alike.

X-Plane

Multiple Releases: 2022 most recent. The leader when it comes to flight dynamics/physics. A popular choice for professional pilots.

Aerofly FS

Released: 2024. One of the only "decent" sims that is actually quite computer-friendly.

Infinite Flight

Released: 2011. A must-have for those that want to fly on a mobile device.
